How to play

How do I form a word?

Swipe across adjacent letter tiles to connect them into a word. You can swipe in any direction — up, down, left, right, and all four diagonals. No tile can be used twice in the same word.

How many waves are there?

Each daily puzzle has six waves. Waves 1–2 are Easy, waves 3–4 are Tricky, and waves 5–6 are Savage. You get one word submission per wave.

What is a Combo?

Successfully submitting a word in consecutive waves builds your combo streak. Each consecutive wave adds a bonus to your score. Miss a wave and your streak resets. Hit all 6 for a Perfect Round and a bonus 200 points.

How is my score calculated?

Your score is based on word length (word length × 10 points) plus a streak bonus that grows with each consecutive successful wave. Longer words on harder waves score the most.

What is slow motion?

Hold your finger anywhere on the screen (not on a tile) to activate slow motion. Everything slows down, giving you more time to think. You have 15 seconds of slow motion per round — use it wisely.

What does the banked time do?

Submit a word before your wave timer runs out and the leftover seconds are distributed equally across your remaining waves. Early submissions help you on the harder later waves.

Why is there only one puzzle per day?

Letter Drop Challenge is a daily game — everyone in the world plays the same puzzle on the same day. Come back tomorrow for a new one.

The app says it can't load today's puzzle — what should I do?

Check your internet connection and try again. The daily puzzle requires a connection to load. If the problem persists, please contact us below.

Why is my streak reset?

Your daily play streak resets if you miss a day. Make sure to play at least once every 24 hours to keep it going.

What does the green word on the results screen mean?

The green word shown next to each wave is the best word available in that grid. Tap it to see its definition and learn something new.
